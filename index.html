Hi, welcome to my webpage!

My name is Corijn and I am a PhD student in pure mathematics at the London School of Geometry and Number Theory, supervised by Netan Dogra. My interests include everything number theory, in particular anything related to solving Diophantine equations. Currently, my work focuses on variations on the method of Chabauty and Coleman for finding rational points on curves.
